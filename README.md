## 1. The Challenge

**Data is the fuel for AI, but the most valuable data remains trapped in silos.**

High-quality, human-labeled data is the essential fuel for training the next generation of powerful AI models. However, the current data supply chain is broken. Centralized labeling platforms are not only inefficient and expensive but also face severe crises of trust.

Data owners holding sensitive information‚Äîsuch as medical records, financial transactions, or private imagery‚Äîhesitate to share data for labeling due to fears of privacy breaches. This stagnation halts AI advancement in critical, high-value sectors.

## 2. The Solution: Label.fi

**Label.fi is a decentralized, trustless data labeling marketplace that redefines how human intelligence securely contributes to AI.**

Drawing inspiration from pioneers like Sapien, we have established a decentralized workforce network. However, we go a fundamental step further by making **privacy preservation** our cornerstone.

Label.fi enables data demanders to publish tasks and allows global labelers (human intelligence) to complete them and earn rewards‚Äîall without any centralized intermediary ever accessing the raw, sensitive data. We are building the "HTTPS for AI data."
